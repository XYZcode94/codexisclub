About CODEXIS

Join us today and experience the magic of coding!

Codexis is a college coding club designed to empower students of all skill levels with practical programming knowledge and real-world problem-solving abilities.
It serves as a hub for collaboration and innovation in areas like AI, web development, and cybersecurity.The club offers a variety of activities, including weekly workshops, Hackathons, mentorship programs, and talks from industry experts. By focusing on collaborative learning and hands-on projects, Codexis aims to make coding accessible and prepare students for future careers in technology.
